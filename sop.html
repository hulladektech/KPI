SOP for KPI Sheet Fill-Up

Step - 1

Fill Out RAW Data with Reports

  • Open the Data Sheet where raw performance data is stored.
    Eg: Master Data Sheet (Team Axis), Lead Management (MOM), Stock Out (Warehouse), Gross Contribution (BD) etc...
  • Cross-check data for completeness and correctness.
Step - 2

Validate Data in the KPI Sheet

  • Open the KPI Sheet (Screenshot given below) and verify information autofetched from Data Sheet .
    Eg: "Name" KPI 24-25
  • Enter the required details into the above KPI Sheet accurately.
    • Eg: Red Cell = Mannual Fill, Green Cell = Auto Fill, Yellow Cell = Mannual Fill (Data from Purchase /Revenue Sheet), Blue Cell = Auto Fill (Checklist Sheet).
  • In case of mannual filling, if target is in %, kindly fill achievement data with % symbol.
  • Recheck the values filled in each column and correct any discrepancies before proceeding.
Step - 3

Access the KPI Rating and Feedback Sheet

  • Click on the link shared on email to open respective team members Rating Sheet.
  • Check the Rating Sheet - KRA-KPI for proper data fetching.
  • Update any incorrect data in the KPI sheet.
  • If you have facing any issues regarding proper data fetching, please contact to IT Team.
Step - 4

Fill Out Additional Sheets

  • Fill the Rating Sheet - Beyond Duty , document extra efforts or achievements of respective team members.
  • Fill the Rating Sheet - Behavioural , provide inputs on professional conducts for respective team members.
Step - 5

Review and Final Feedback

  • Open the Performance Summary & Feedback Sheet.
  • Ensure scores in all parameters are correctly fetched.
  • Mannualy filled the score in the column towards Self Development
  • Complete your feedback, comments, and observations in the feedback section.
  • Recheck, save and submit the sheet

Final Checks:


✔ Verify no fields are left blank.
✔ Confirm feedback is relevant and actionable.
✔ Sheet is Properly Calculated.
✔ Submit the completed sheet on time.
