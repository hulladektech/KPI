SOP for KPI Sheet Fill-Up

Highlight Section

Step 1: Fill Out RAW Data with Reports

  • Open the Data Sheet where raw performance data is stored.
    Eg: Master Data Sheet (Team Axis), Lead Management (MOM), Stock Out (Warehouse), Gross Contribution (BD) etc...
  • Enter the required details into the KPI Sheet accurately.
  • Cross-check data for completeness and correctness.
Highlight Section

Step 2: Validate Data in the KPI Sheet

  • Open the KPI Sheet and verify fetched data.
    Eg: "Name" KPI 24-25
  • Ensure values match corresponding entries in the source data.
  • Correct any discrepancies before proceeding.
Highlight Section

Step 3: Access the Feedback Sheet

  • Click on the mail link to open the Feedback Sheet.
  • Check the KRA-KPI Subsheet for proper data fetching.
  • Update any incorrect data in the KPI sheet.
Highlight Section

Step 4: Fill Out Additional Sheets

  • Beyond Duty Sheet: Document extra efforts or achievements.
  • Behavioural Sheet: Provide inputs on professional conduct.
Highlight Section

Step 5: Review and Final Feedback

  • Review the Performance Summary Sheet.
  • Ensure all sections are correctly populated.
  • Write down feedback, comments, and observations.
  • Save and submit the sheet as per workflow.
Highlight Section

Final Checks:

  • ✔ Ensure data integrity in all sheets.
  • ✔ Verify no fields are left blank.
  • ✔ Confirm feedback is relevant and actionable.
  • ✔ Sheet is Properly Calculated.
  • ✔ Submit the completed sheet on time.
